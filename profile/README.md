## AI for people who work in travel and hospitality 👋

[inHotel](https://www.inhotel.io) builds AI assistants, AI personas, and agent skills for hotels and the wider travel industry.

### AI assistants

Our assistants are designed around real hospitality roles. Guest relations, reservations, revenue, marketing, housekeeping, finance, and management all have different pressures and different kinds of knowledge. A hotel can start with its own material, then add connections or capabilities as it learns what is useful.

### AI personas

People leave jobs. Knowledge often leaves with them. We think it should not have to.

An AI persona, or digital twin, gives a hospitality professional a way to share their knowledge when they are not in the room. It can carry their perspective into a conversation, point people back to their work, and stay useful after a meeting or a career move is over.

### AI agent skills

We call a focused piece of work an agent skill. It might guide a team through a procedure, analyze guest feedback, help with a revenue decision, handle a complex negotiation, or draft a newsletter. Skills enhance AI assistants and personas with specialized capabilities.

The [inHotel Agent Skills Catalog](https://www.inhotel.io/agent-skills/catalog) is a place to browse these capabilities. Hospitality experts, operators, and technology partners can contribute skills and make them available to the assistants that need them.

If you know a workflow well enough to explain it, you can [build an agent skill with inHotel](https://www.inhotel.io/agent-skills/build). You can do the work yourself or collaborate with us on the process design and engineering.

A workflow can stay private inside one organization or become an asset that earns its owner money when other organizations use it. This way, companies can turn IT costs into IT revenue.

## GoVisit.ai

[GoVisit.ai](https://govisit.ai) is the part of inHotel that connects travel planners with hospitality businesses in destinations and facilitates direct bookings.

Hotels, restaurants, tour and experience operators, landmarks, golf courses, and spas need visibility across AI search platforms and planning agents. GoVisit’s Places enables businesses to avoid costly website rebuild projects and instead adopt a content-rich, structured profile page that makes them AI-ready in minutes.

Destinations need more than a list of businesses. A city or region also needs local context, collections, and planning information. GoVisit brings those pieces together so someone can decide what to do next.

Visit [GoVisit.ai](https://govisit.ai) to see the travel-facing side of our work.

## Why we support open source

Software is more useful when people can inspect it, learn from it, and improve it. We publish code, specifications, documentation, and reusable pieces when the work allows it.

Not every customer project can be public. That is normal. We can still share the ideas, interfaces, and tools that others may find useful. Smaller teams and independent builders should not need a large budget just to understand how a system works.

## For developers

There is no single framework or product here. Some repositories are rough experiments. Others are closer to production. Most started with a problem someone in the industry had actually run into.

If you work on AI agents, hospitality software, geospatial identity, destination systems, structured data, or open source, have a look around. Issues, questions, and contributions are welcome.
